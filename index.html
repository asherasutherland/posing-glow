About

Posing Glow is a quasi-label and music library. First and foremost, it's a low-stakes place for people to release what they've made, without any of the usual label games. It works a bit like a community book exchange: everything's free to download, and if you take something, you're encouraged to leave something of your own. It's about treating music as something worth sharing. Submissions always open.
